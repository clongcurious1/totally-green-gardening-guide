Identify Your Growing Conditions

Your garden site is unique based on the hours of daylight it receives and how well the soil drains.

From the options below, select the description of your growing site.

"Sunny" sites receive 6-or-more hours of strong sunlight per day.

"Shady" sites receive less than 6 hours of direct sunlight per day.

Your growing conditions determine your Totally Green garden style and specific No-Fail plants.
